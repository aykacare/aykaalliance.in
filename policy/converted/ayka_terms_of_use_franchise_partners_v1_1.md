## 3. FRANCHISE TIERS AND STRUCTURE
3.1  The AYKA Alliance franchise network comprises three tiers as follows:
- City Franchise Partner: Responsible for direct outreach, onboarding, and support of doctors, clinics, and hospitals within the assigned city territory. Investment and revenue structure as per Franchise Agreement.
